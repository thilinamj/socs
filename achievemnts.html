letmeHack 2018

LetmeHack 2018 was the first ever product oriented hackathon in Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka which is organized with the key idea of building up a complete open source product which can be used by everyone. This event was planned as a 16-hour product implementation hackathon.20 teams representing different universities participated the event. Participants had to implement a product according to the platform given by the organizing team. The implemented product should be a useful outcome and it will be judged by a team of technological experts.













Virtual Rival

Virtual Rival is organized by Society of Computer Sciences in collaboration with department of Computing and Information Systems. It is a gaming competition that focuses on the gaming skills, planning, improving new innovative ideas and team work of undergraduates as they are the future generation who will take part in the IT industry of Sri Lanka in future. several teams take part in the competition and winners are awarded with gifts as an encouragement.Currently organized by the second year cis students in the university.Team work of the students highly exhibited throughout all the event.
